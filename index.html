Why ICITSIF 2026?

The International Conference on Intelligent Technologies for a Sustainable and Inclusive Future (ICITSIF 2026) is organized by the IEEE WIE Affinity Group. This conference brings together thought leaders, researchers, and practitioners from across the globe to explore cutting-edge technologies that are shaping sustainable development and inclusive growth.

With a strong emphasis on diversity and inclusion, ICITSIF 2026 highlights the vital role of women in technology and STEM leadership. The conference features keynote talks, paper presentations, panel discussions, and hands-on workshops spanning 11 critical technology domains.

Whether you're a seasoned researcher, a graduate student, or an industry professional, ICITSIF 2026 offers a unique opportunity to present your work, learn from experts, network with peers, and contribute to building a sustainable and inclusive future.

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research papers that fall within the scope of the conference tracks. All submissions will undergo a rigorous double-blind peer review process by the Technical Program Committee.

  • Papers must be written in English and formatted according to the IEEE conference template.
  • Full papers should be 4–6 pages in length (including references).
  • All accepted and presented papers will be submitted for potential inclusion in IEEE Xplore.
  • At least one author must register and present the paper at the conference.
  • Submissions will be reviewed on the basis of originality, technical quality, relevance, and clarity.
  • Plagiarism in any form is strictly prohibited and will result in rejection.
